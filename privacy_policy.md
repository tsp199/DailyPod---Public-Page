Privacy Policy - DailyPod
Effective Date: January 24, 2026
Last Updated: January 24, 2026

DailyPod ("DailyPod," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and stored when you use the DailyPod iOS app (the "App").
By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1) What DailyPod Collects and Stores

A) Data Stored on Our Server (required for app functionality)
DailyPod requires an account to use playlists and saved shows. When you register and use the App, we store:
- Account info: Apple sign-in identifier and email (if provided)
- Playlists: names, ordering mode, and which shows are included
- Saved playlist: your default Saved playlist (unique per user)
- Podcast data: show metadata and episodes the app has pulled in so they can appear in playlists

B) Data Stored Locally on Your Device
Playback Progress (local):
- Episode audio URL (identifier)
- Last listened position (seconds)
- Duration (seconds, when available)
- Completion status

Auth Token (local):
- The app stores your login token in local device storage so you don’t have to log in every time.

Recently Played History (in-app session):
- A temporary history list is kept in memory while the app is running to support "Skip to Previous Episode."
- This is not persisted and resets when the app restarts.

C) Data DailyPod Does Not Collect (Based on Current Code)
DailyPod does not:
- collect your phone number
- access your contacts, photos, microphone, or camera
- record audio
- track precise GPS location
- collect payment information

2) Podcast Index & Network Requests
The App communicates with the DailyPod backend, which proxies requests to the Podcast Index API for search and recommendations.
- Your device will send network requests to our server when you search, load recommendations, or load playlists.
- Requests include your auth token for account access.

3) Now Playing / Lock Screen
DailyPod uses Apple’s media playback APIs to display on the lock screen and Control Center:
- episode title
- show title
- duration
- playback position
- artwork (if available)
This data is used only for playback controls and metadata display on your device.

4) Podcast Artwork Loading
DailyPod may download podcast artwork from URLs associated with the show.
- This causes network requests for images
- iOS may cache artwork as part of normal system behavior
- DailyPod does not upload your photos or personal media

5) Advertising (Not Enabled Yet)
DailyPod does not currently show ads.
If ads are added in the future (e.g., AdMob), third-party ad providers may collect information such as device identifiers, approximate location (based on IP), and ad performance data.
We will update this policy before enabling ads.

6) How We Use Information
DailyPod uses stored data to:
- authenticate your account
- store and retrieve your playlists
- resume playback and track progress
- enable recommendations based on your playlist content

7) Sharing of Data
DailyPod does not sell your personal information.
We do not share playback progress with third parties.
If we add third-party services (ads, analytics, crash reporting), we will update this policy.

8) Data Retention & Deletion
- Server data (account + playlists) is retained until you delete your account (feature not yet available).
- Local playback progress is retained unless you uninstall the App.

9) Children’s Privacy
DailyPod is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above will reflect the latest version.
