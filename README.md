# DailyPod
The Public Page for the app DailyPod

# About
DailyPod is your new favorite Podcast listening app. Built off of the Podcasting Index (https://podcastindex.org), DailyPod gives you the ability to create custom playlists by shows, rather than by episode.

Pick back up right where you left off. Always listen to the latest episodes. Or catch up on your favorite road trip shows starting with the oldest episode you haven't listened to yet.

No matter how you want to listen to your shows, DailyPod is here for you.
